Meet The All-Stars

Four funk-fuelled hearts, one sleazy groove machine.

Born out of the sticky floors and smoky back rooms of Manchester’s forgotten funk haunts, The Mattress All-Stars bring the soundtrack of 70s adult cinema to life — thick bass, screaming brass, and rhythms that make the wallpaper sweat.

Broadcasting from LoveJamFM 99.69, we are the North West’s premier purveyors of unapologetic, toe-curling live funk. We’re here to make your hips move, your neighbours complain, and your soul feel just a little bit naughty.

Meet the Groove Dealers

Jack "The Low-End Lothario"

Bass

Our founding bassist, bringing lines so deep they should come with a safety warning. Knows three ways to make your furniture vibrate without touching it.

Iain "The Six-String Casanova"

Guitar

Turns every riff into a seduction and every solo into a confession. If there’s a wah pedal nearby, he’s already standing on it.

Lee "The Percussion Predator"

Drums

Hitting things rhythmically for over two decades. Capable of locking a groove so tight it could get banned in six states.

Matt "Brass Daddy"

Trombone

Blasts so hot they could melt polyester. Rumour has it he once caused a pair of flared trousers to combust mid-set.
